üåü Key Visual Features

  • Vision Board Centerpiece: Large, beautiful vision board is the main focus of the app
  • Progressive Reveal: Journaling unlocks more of your vision board, creating anticipation
  • Inspirational Imagery: Every page uses beautiful photos and visual metaphors for goals
  • Masonry Layouts: Pinterest-style layouts for visual interest and optimal image display
  • Daily Quotes: Rotating inspirational quotes to maintain motivation
  • Milestone Gallery: Visual celebration of achievements with images
  • Photo Journaling: Easy ability to attach photos to journal entries
  • Minimal UI: Clean, uncluttered interface that lets the imagery shine
  • Smooth Animations: Gentle transitions and reveals for a premium feel

üé® Design Principles

  • Beauty inspires action - every screen should be visually appealing
  • Progress is visual - use imagery and animations to show advancement
  • Less is more - minimal UI elements, maximum visual impact
  • Celebrate milestones - make achievements feel special with visual rewards
  • Personal connection - user's own images and goals take center stage
